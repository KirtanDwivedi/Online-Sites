What is Work Ethic?

A strong work ethic is not just about working hard. It's a foundational set of moral principles and standards that guide your behavior and beliefs in the workplace. It encompasses your attitude, sense of responsibility, and how you interact with colleagues, clients, and the company culture as a whole.

The Ethical Framework: Navigating Right and Wrong

Moral Values

These are the personal standards of good and bad that govern our behavior. Largely shaped by our upbringing, society, and personal beliefs, they form our internal compass.

Ethics

Derived from the Greek "Ethos" (character), ethics are the external, shared principles that guide the conduct of a group or profession. They provide a structured set of rules for action.

Classifying Professional Actions

Ethical & Legal

The ideal standard for all professional conduct.

Unethical & Legal

Actions that are not illegal but violate moral or professional standards.

Ethical & Illegal

Rare situations where a moral obligation may conflict with the law.

Unethical & Illegal

Clearly wrong and punishable by law and professional bodies.
